CapFinder Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2025

1. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

CapFinder does not collect any personal information from users.

2. Personal Information Collected

CapFinder does not collect any personal information.

  • No account information collection
  • No login information collection
  • No location information collection
  • No device information collection
  • No user behavior tracking

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to external servers.

  • Keycap information is included within the app
  • User settings are stored only on the device

4. Image Loading

The app may load images from external URLs to display keycap images.

  • Image URLs are included in the app's internal data
  • No cookies or other tracking technologies are used for user tracking
  • Images are cached and can be viewed offline

5. Third-Party Services

CapFinder does not use third-party analytics services or advertising networks.

6. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no retention or use period.

7. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no information provided to third parties.

8. Privacy Officer

CapFinder does not have a separate privacy officer since we do not collect personal information.

9. Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be changed in accordance with changes in laws, services, or internal policies. Changes will be posted on this page, and for significant changes, notifications may be provided within the app.
